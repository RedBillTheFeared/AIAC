About AIAC LKO

Crafting architectural excellence since 2009

Architectural Innovations & Construction (AIAC LKO) is a leading architecture and construction firm based in Lucknow, specializing in sustainable and innovative design solutions. Founded by Chief Architect Ahmed Haroon, we have been transforming visions into architectural masterpieces for over a decade.

Our expertise spans across architectural design, interior design, landscape architecture, and comprehensive project management. As an International Associate of the American Institute of Architects and IGBC Accredited Professional, we bring global standards to local projects.
